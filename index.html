Our Legacy

Discover the legacy and mission of 2 TN Air Squadron NCC, where young minds are shaped into future leaders of our nation.

Our Legacy

The 2 TN Air Squadron NCC (Coimbatore Group) has been a cornerstone of youth development in Tamil Nadu, dedicated to nurturing discipline, leadership, and patriotic spirit among college students since its establishment.

As part of the prestigious Air Wing of the National Cadet Corps, we provide unique opportunities for cadets to experience military training, aviation exposure, and character development that shapes them into responsible citizens and future leaders.

Our squadron operates under the motto "Unity & Discipline," emphasizing the core values that guide every cadet's journey from enrollment to graduation.

What Makes Us Special

  • ✈️ Air Wing Training: Specialized aviation training and aircraft familiarization programs
  • 👑 Leadership Development: Comprehensive programs to build command and management skills
  • 🌍 Social Service: Community outreach and nation-building activities
  • 🎖️ Career Benefits: NCC 'C' certificate advantages in defense and government services

About Our ANO

Meet the dedicated Associate NCC Officer who guides our squadron with vision and commitment.

Our Associate NCC Officer

Our Associate NCC Officer (ANO) is the backbone of 2 TN Air Squadron NCC, leading with dedication and inspiring cadets to achieve excellence. With extensive experience in training and mentoring, our ANO brings a wealth of knowledge in military discipline, aviation, and leadership development.

Under her guidance, cadets undergo rigorous training programs that instill values of unity, discipline, and service. Her commitment to youth development and nation-building ensures that every cadet is prepared for both personal and professional success.

  • 🎓 Expert in NCC training methodologies
