Our Legacy

The 2 TN Air Squadron NCC (Coimbatore Group) has been a cornerstone of youth development in Tamil Nadu, dedicated to nurturing discipline, leadership, and patriotic spirit among college students since its establishment.

As part of the prestigious Air Wing of the National Cadet Corps, we provide unique opportunities for cadets to experience military training, aviation exposure, and character development that shapes them into responsible citizens and future leaders.

Our squadron operates under the motto "Unity & Discipline," emphasizing the core values that guide every cadet's journey from enrollment to graduation.
